Purdue Boilermakers Big Ten Mackey Arena's booming atmosphere sparks offensive firepower and gets a favorable whistle.

Measuring peak sound levels of up to 124.3 decibels, Purdue's Mackey Arena is one of the loudest venues teams will ever face. The arena's bowl shape creates a tightly packed crowd, making it easy for fans to get in opponents' heads. While this atmosphere gives the Boilermakers a solid defensive boost, the real advantage comes on the offensive end. Since 2015, their offensive rating increases by 14 points and their true shooting by six percentage points on average while at Mackey. Most notably, the team draws nearly five more fouls than their opponents per game at home, which is the most out of all Power Five teams.

Brigham Young Cougars Big 12 Marriott Center's hostile fans intimidate opposing free throw shooters and fuel lockdown defense.

BYU boasts a strong fan base at Marriott Center, ranking seventh in Division I in average attendance with 17,054 fans per game. The team's student section, dubbed as "The Roar of Cougars," is known for being an intimidating presence on opponents, especially when they step to the free throw stripe. Fans can be seen jumping in unison as the opponent goes up to shoot, all while waving large inflatables and dizzying signs. Over the past ten seasons, BYU has held its opponenets to a mere 67% scoring rate on free throws, which ranks first among Power Five teams.

Duke Blue Devils ACC Cameron Indoor Stadium's electric student section bolsters team energy while destroying opponents' morale.

Getting the opportunity to watch a game in Duke's Cameron Indoor Stadium is as cutthroat as it gets. The venue has sold out 594 consecutive games, which dates back to 1990, and the Blue Devils have won over 90% of the games in that stretch. To attend Duke's biggest games of the season, students camp outside the arena for days just to get a shot at a ticket. The student section, known as the "Cameron Crazies", is uniquely located alongside the court, giving fans closer proximity for heckling opponents and cheering on their players.
